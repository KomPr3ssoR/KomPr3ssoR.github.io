Data You Need to Know

Objective term

Personally identifiable information (PII)

Protected health information (PHI)

EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

  • Strict laws govern what organizations can do with PHI
  • The GDPR in the EU specifies how and what data online organizations can get from visitors
  • The payment card industry follows the PCI DDS to ensure security of financial interactions
